Uncle Ramesh: Census Critics Don't Understand How Government Works

January 13, 2026

Uncle Ramesh’s Take#

Straight Talk from Brooklyn to Georgetown

Let me break it down for you.

üìä CENSUS TOOK 4 YEARS? THAT’S CALLED BEING THOROUGH!#

All these critics crying “4 years is too long!” You know what I say? Quality over speed!

878,674 people on census day 2022
956,044 projected by end 2024

You think you just count people and done? NO!

You have to:

  • Verify the data (multiple times!)
  • Cross-check everything
  • Make sure numbers accurate
  • Quality control
  • Double-check
  • Triple-check

These opposition people want us to rush and give wrong numbers? Then they’ll complain the numbers are wrong! You can’t win with these people!

Better to take 4 years and get it RIGHT than rush and get it WRONG.

And you know what those numbers show? Population GROWING! From 878,000 to 956,000! People STAYING in Guyana instead of running away like they did under PNC!

That’s the real story! But Stabroek News won’t tell you that!

And these opposition people want to make THIS man Leader of the Opposition? With access to state secrets? With security briefings? With representing Guyana internationally?

Are you CRAZY?

Making sure everything proper and legal before installing someone facing extradition as Opposition Leader.

Just one! Is that too much to ask?

You know what’s happening now? Trump talking about flooding the market with Venezuelan oil. Prices going DOWN!

You know what’s coming in 10-15 years? Electric cars everywhere! Oil demand CRASHING!

So these critics want us to sit on our oil for 20 years waiting for what? For prices to crash? For demand to disappear? For the market to die?

NO THANK YOU!

Simple economics:

Option A (Critics’ way):
Pump slow ‚Üí Market crashes ‚Üí Oil worthless ‚Üí We broke

Option B (Government way):
Pump fast ‚Üí Get money NOW ‚Üí Build economy ‚Üí Ready for future

Which makes sense? OPTION B!

And look what we building with the oil money:

  • New roads everywhere
  • Bridges connecting regions
  • Schools and hospitals
  • Gas-to-Energy (cheaper electricity coming!)
  • Housing developments

That’s investing in the future! That’s what smart governments do!

TOO FAST?

You can’t win with these opposition people!

Planning permission is serious! You can’t just open a business anywhere you want. There are rules! Infrastructure! Community impact!

CH&PA did their JOB. They enforced the LAW. That’s what they supposed to do!

But where was this energy with the 87% of businesses violating consumer laws?

Fair question! And you know what? The CCAC is WORKING on it!

506 complaints filed! $156 MILLION recovered! Systems being built!

You want them to fix 87% overnight? That’s not realistic!

But at least NOW there’s a system. At least NOW consumers can fight back. Under PNC, there was NOTHING!

Progress takes time. But it’s happening!

  • Cooperation
  • Partnership

Sometimes you have to work with what you got. And we got a chance with Exxon. We took it. Now we benefiting!

And it’s WORKING!
