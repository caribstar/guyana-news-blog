## 🎯 The Big Story: Budget Consultations - Private Sector Yes, Opposition Nah

The government is making the rounds consulting on Budget 2026, meeting with the **Private Sector Commission**, **Georgetown Chamber of Commerce**, and every business group that owns a tie. The PSC is asking for lower taxes on equipment, more housing incentives, and probably a pony.

Meanwhile, the **PNCR** is complaining they weren't invited to consultations. Finance Minister Ashni Singh essentially said the opposition had "no meaningful economic policies" to contribute. 

Translation: "We asked what you'd do with the money and you said 'investigate the PPP,' so... we're good."

---

## 🗺️ Venezuela: The Map That Keeps On Giving

The **Venezuela situation** continues to provide entertainment. President Maduro is still claiming Essequibo on maps while **CARICOM**, the **OAS**, and basically everyone except Maduro's barber has condemned the annexation claims.

In related news, the US has now officially backed Guyana's territorial integrity. Nothing says "we support you" like an American warship casually passing through your waters.

Venezuela responded by saying the US is "imperialist." In other news, water is wet.

---

## 💰 NIS: Where Did $10 Billion Go?

**Kaieteur News** is asking what happened to the $10 billion "injection" into the NIS that was announced but never quite explained. The pension fund is running deficits while pensioners are getting increases that barely cover the cost of one extra roti per month.

The government says the fund is "sustainable." Pensioners say their grocery bills disagree.

---

## 🎾 Sports: Guyana Women Blaze Past Jamaica

The **Guyana Amazon Warriors Women** demolished Jamaica in the T20 Blaze tournament! **Shemaine Campbelle** scored a brilliant 50* as Guyana cruised to victory.

Nice to see Guyana winning something that doesn't require a court case or an international tribunal.

---

## 🚨 Crime Corner: Security Guards With Toy Guns

Police arrested security guards who were supposed to be protecting a business with **airsoft guns** instead of real firearms. Yes, you read that right. The guards were essentially armed with expensive toys.

When criminals show up, were they planning to yell "pew pew pew" really loudly?

The company is now under investigation. So is whoever thought this was a good idea.

---

## 🏗️ Infrastructure Update: $94M Day Care Still Not Open

Remember that **$94 million day care facility** that had a ribbon-cutting ceremony five months ago? Still not operational. The building is complete, the furniture is in, but apparently actually opening requires... something. Nobody's quite sure what.

Parents are still waiting. The ribbon, however, has been thoroughly cut.
