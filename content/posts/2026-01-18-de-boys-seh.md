*Every Sunday, we check in with the boys at Rudy's Corner Shop for the real talk.*

**Present:** Big Man (taxi driver), Shorty (construction), Specs (government clerk), Rambo (hustler)

---

**Big Man:** Before we talk anything else — the women's cricket team!

**Rambo:** TWO wins! Undefeated!

**Shorty:** Campbelle score 50 not out against Jamaica. CAPTAIN innings!

**Specs:** Defending champions looking like champions for real.

**Big Man:** You know what sweet me? While everybody talking about men's team drama, the women just quietly WINNING.

**Rambo:** Facts. No controversy. No politics. Just cricket.

---

**Shorty:** But speaking of drama — Fruta Conquerors president resign!

**Specs:** Martin Massiah. Eleven months only.

**Big Man:** Young fella too. Thirty years old. Thought he was going change things.

**Rambo:** He DID change things. Digital transformation, merchandise, stadium upgrades. Then he gone.

**Shorty:** Running a football club ain't easy. Elite League is STRESS.

**Big Man:** Meanwhile, six Guyanese get FIFA badges. Shavin Greene promoted to Tier 2!

**Rambo:** Wait, we producing international referees now?

**Specs:** Kleon Lindey on FIFA List for TWELVE years straight!

**Shorty:** So we can't run a club properly but we producing world-class refs? Make it make sense!

---

**Big Man:** Alright, the banking thing. GBTI launching "private banking."

**Rambo:** Private how? They does lock the door?

**Specs:** Nah, is banking for rich people. Investment advice, dedicated banker, portfolio management...

**Shorty:** So if you rich, you get a whole PERSON assigned to you? I can't even get the teller to make eye contact!

**Big Man:** Same speech, Ali say banks should go bus park help minibus drivers open accounts.

**Rambo:** *(laughing)* "Good afternoon driver, want a savings account with your chicken foot?"

**Shorty:** Rich man in air condition office. Poor man getting financial literacy next to the doubles cart.

**Big Man:** Both called "inclusive banking." 

**All:** *(skeptical noises)*

---

**Specs:** The hotel situation getting wild though. ANOTHER US$18 million one open.

**Rambo:** Plaza Court. Rooftop bar. Presidential suite.

**Shorty:** Presidential suite for who? 

**Big Man:** Tourists. And tourism up 22%, they say.

**Rambo:** You know what hotel mean for regular man? JOBS. But you working IN the presidential suite, not staying in it.

**Specs:** That's how economy work though. Money trickle...

**Shorty:** *(interrupting)* You ever stand under a trickle? You know how LONG you waiting to get wet?

---

**Big Man:** Health records going digital now.

**Rambo:** My auntie went clinic yesterday. THREE hours wait. Two different lines.

**Specs:** Is transition period...

**Shorty:** Transition to what? Confusion?

**Big Man:** But real talk, paper records was disaster too. You ever try get file transferred? Like treasure hunt!

**Rambo:** True. My grandmother file LOST three times. 

**Specs:** See? Digital fixing that. Eventually.

**Shorty:** "Eventually" is Guyana's middle name.

---

**Big Man:** Kaieteur asking if we vassal state to Exxon.

**Rambo:** What's a vassal?

**Shorty:** Servant country. Doing what big man say.

**Specs:** That's too harsh. We getting development...

**Rambo:** With WE oil money! Two percent royalty while they taking 75% for "costs."

**Big Man:** Sound like when I was driving taxi for the old man. He take most, I do all the work!

**Shorty:** The country is a taxi driver working commission!

---

**Specs:** Mashramani launch though. 

**Big Man:** Yeah, we need SOMETHING to celebrate.

**Rambo:** Celebrate the women's cricket team! They actually WINNING!

**Shorty:** Facts. Monday they playing Trinidad. I taking the day off.

**Big Man:** You taking day off for cricket?

**Shorty:** For WOMEN'S cricket. The men does just stress me out. The women does make me PROUD.

---

**Big Man:** Final words?

**Shorty:** Women's team showing how it done. No excuses, just results.

**Specs:** Progress messy but happening. Patience required.

**Rambo:** Private banking for rich man, bus park for poor man. Same "inclusive" economy.

**Big Man:** Me? I watching that T20 match Monday. And when them women win again, remember — they doing it without the drama, without the politics, just EXCELLENCE.

**All:** Facts!

---

*De Boys Seh runs every Sunday.*
