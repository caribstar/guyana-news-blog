SOESDYKE — A Ministry of Public Works spokesperson confirmed Wednesday that the long-anticipated upgrade of the Georgetown-Linden corridor remains in the planning stage, a status the project has maintained continuously since 2012, with engineers now reportedly reviewing revised proposals to revise the previous revisions.

The situation continues to develop.
