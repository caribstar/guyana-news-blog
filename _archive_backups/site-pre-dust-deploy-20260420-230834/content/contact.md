## Get in touch

We read everything that comes in. We don't always respond quickly, but we do read.

**Email:** hello@tradewindsbrief.com

## News tips

Got a story we should cover? Something the papers missed, something happening in your community, something you wish someone would look into?

Use the subject line: **News Tip — [brief description]**

Please include:

- What happened and where
- Whether you're comfortable being named (we'll ask again before publishing anything)
- Any documents, photos, or links that support what you're telling us

We protect sources. We do not share who sent us what, under any condition, with any third party.

## Feedback on coverage

Got thoughts on something we wrote? A correction? A disagreement? A better way to frame something?

Use the subject line: **Feedback — [article title]**

We read feedback with more care than most publications. If we got something wrong, we want to know.
