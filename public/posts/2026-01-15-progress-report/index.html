📈 Weekly Progress Report: January 8-15, 2026

January 15, 2026 · 4 min · 651 words · CARIBSTAR
From The Tradewinds Brief — daily diaspora reporting. Subscribe Free →

This is a weekly factual report documenting PPP/C government achievements, initiatives, and progress. No political commentary—just verifiable accomplishments.

🏗️ Infrastructure & Development#

Airstrip Rehabilitation Program: DELIVERING RESULTS#

Achievement: Multiple airlines reduce fares following government’s airstrip rehabilitation completion

  • Trans Guyana Airways: 7% fare reduction
  • Air Services Limited: 9% fare reduction
  • Direct flights to Paramakatoi now operating 3x weekly

Impact: Lower travel costs for hinterland communities, improved accessibility

Status: ✅ COMPLETED & DELIVERING BENEFITS

Search & Rescue Modernization: $123M Investment#

Achievement: New Search and Rescue Information and Management System (SARIMS) launched at Timehri Control Tower

  • 16 personnel trained by Canadian experts
  • Real-time data integration operational
  • Georgetown Rescue Coordination Centre established

Impact: Faster emergency response, increased safety for air and maritime operations

Status: ✅ OPERATIONAL

⚖️ Justice System Improvements#

Court Backlog Reduction: 5-Year Sustained Progress#

Achievement: January 2026 Demerara Criminal Assizes lists 126 cases (down from hundreds)

  • Backlog declining consistently for 5 consecutive years
  • Major historical cases finally proceeding to trial
  • DPP confirms sustained investment yielding results

Impact: Faster justice delivery, reduced waiting times for victims and accused

Status: 🔄 ONGOING PROGRESS

🏘️ Housing Development#

Housing Stock Expansion: 92,233 New Structures Built#

Achievement: Between 2012-2022, government facilitated construction of 92,233 new structures

  • Building stock increased 42%
  • Households increased 32.9%
  • Forward planning for population growth

Impact: Reduced housing shortage, more Guyanese owning homes

Status: ✅ DOCUMENTED IN CENSUS DATA

💼 Economic Growth Indicators#

Population Growth: Evidence of Economic Confidence#

Achievement: Population reached 878,674 (preliminary census), estimated 956,044 by end of 2024

  • 17.6% increase since 2012
  • Foreign-born residents nearly tripled (economic opportunity attracting migrants)
  • More males than females: 440,062 men to 432,608 women

Impact: Growing workforce, economic expansion, demographic confidence

Status: ✅ CENSUS CONFIRMED

🛡️ National Security#

CANU Drug Interdiction: Enhanced Capabilities#

Achievement: 2025 operational gains documented

  • Major drug seizures throughout year
  • National Early Warning System launched (detect new psychoactive substances)
  • 27 training programmes completed
  • Enhanced regional and international cooperation

Impact: Better equipped to fight transnational crime, improved intelligence capacity

Status: ✅ 2025 OBJECTIVES MET

👮 Law Enforcement#

Traffic Enforcement: System Functioning#

Achievement: 3,107 traffic violations recorded in one week (Jan 4-10, 2026)

  • 964 speeders caught
  • 168 helmetless motorcyclists stopped
  • Enhanced detection methods and technology deployed

Impact: Safer roads through active enforcement, deterrent effect

Status: 🔄 ONGOING ENFORCEMENT

🌾 Agricultural Leadership#

Regional Prominence: Guyanese Leading IICA#

Achievement: Muhammad Ibrahim assumes role as Director-General of Inter-American Institute for Cooperation on Agriculture (IICA)

  • First Guyanese to hold this position
  • Regional recognition of Guyana’s agricultural expertise
  • Platform for advancing Guyanese agricultural interests regionally

Impact: Enhanced regional influence, agricultural development opportunities

Status: ✅ APPOINTMENT CONFIRMED

💰 Financial System Modernization#

Commitment to Reform: President Ali Announces Initiative#

Achievement: Financial system modernization announced, including stock exchange upgrade

  • Aims to adapt financial framework to growing economy
  • Financial inclusion initiatives planned
  • Investment opportunities for ordinary Guyanese

Impact: (Pending implementation)

Status: 🔄 ANNOUNCED, IMPLEMENTATION PENDING

This Week’s Documented Achievements:

  • ✅ 2 major infrastructure completions delivering benefits (airstrips, SARIMS)
  • ✅ 5-year sustained progress on court backlog
  • ✅ 92,233 new structures built (census data)
  • ✅ Population growth of 17.6% since 2012
  • ✅ Enhanced drug interdiction capabilities
  • ✅ Active traffic law enforcement
  • ✅ Guyanese leading major regional organization
  • 🔄 Financial modernization announced

Completion Rate: 7 of 8 items completed or showing measurable progress (87.5%)

  2. Justice system improving through sustained investment (5-year backlog reduction)
  3. Economic confidence growing (population increase, foreign-born residents tripling)
  4. Regional influence expanding (IICA leadership)
