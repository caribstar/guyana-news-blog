# TRADEWINDS BRIEF — CANON

Date locked: 2026-04-23

## Positioning statement

> We dont report the news. We explain what it means — and show you how its being spun.

## What the site is

- A diaspora context product that uses news as raw material
- A signal amplifier + interpreter, not a newsroom
- Raw news = commodity. Context + framing = product. Voice + tension (duel) = differentiation

## Content mix (directional, not rigid)

Target: 70% news / 20% commentary / 10% satire over time.

Current archive reality: mostly commentary + parody_column. New news content fills the news tier going forward. No bulk upgrades.

## Three-tier production system

**Tier 1 — Fast pipeline (daily):**
- AI-assisted aggregation from Reuters / AP / local papers / government releases / X signals
- Facts extracted, not copied
- Structured rewrite into THE NEWS + WHY IT MATTERS format
- Powers homepage volume + duel strip

**Tier 2 — Voice layer (identity + shareability):**
- Uncle Ramesh (pro-gov loyalist, parody_column)
- Bro Winston (anti-gov Rasta intellectual, parody_column)
- Other character columns as franchises

**Tier 3 — Premium depth (rare, 1-2 per week max):**
- Explainer pieces
- "How this actually affects you" features
- Must not bleed into daily output. Ever.

## THE CARD RULE (non-negotiable)

Every news card shows:

```
HEADLINE
THE FACTS: (1 line)
WHY IT MATTERS: (2 lines)
```

If a card does not communicate WHY IT MATTERS at a glance, it is not publish-ready.

## THE WHY IT MATTERS RULE

Every WHY IT MATTERS must contain three elements:

1. **Mechanism** — why this causes that
2. **Condition** — "if this holds", "if the pattern continues"
3. **Timeframe** — "in the coming weeks", "over the next few months"

Not hedging. Structured interpretation.

Bad: "Prices will rise soon."

Good: "Currency drops usually push up import costs — if this continues, expect higher prices on imported goods in the coming weeks."

## THE 10-SECOND RULE

A reader landing on the homepage must understand, without clicking:
- what happened
- why it matters
- what the argument is

If they need to click to get these three things, we have already lost them.

## Production rules (hard, non-negotiable)

1. **No sentence reuse.** If a sentence appears in the source, it does not appear in the article. Not lightly rewritten. Reconstructed.
2. **Facts extracted, not copied.** Read source, extract facts as bullets, rebuild in own structure. Never copy-tweak-publish.
3. **Source always visible.** Not conditional. Every news item shows source on the card or at the top of the article.
4. **Add value before publish.** Each item must include at least one: diaspora framing (preferred), economic implication, or behavioral implication. If none, do not publish.

## THE DUEL STRIP RULE

REALITY CHECK format: THE NEWS (factual, NEWS badge) paired with THE TAKE (satirical, SATIRE badge).

The satire side must survive being screenshotted alone. Every THE TAKE must have all three visible signals at once:

1. Character name (first line, bold)
2. SATIRE / PARODY badge (high contrast, not subtle)
3. Tone-consistent voice (immediately recognizable in first sentence)

Missing any one = do not publish that duel.

Use sparingly: 2-3 pairs max per homepage. Must feel curated, not chaotic.

## Content type ladder (locked)

- `news` — ONLY posts with full WHY IT MATTERS structure (new system only)
- `commentary` — Daily Brief + analysis + legacy aggregation + Speedeet & Wilar + News / Regional News / Opinion / Response / Caribbean Brief
- `parody_column` — all character-driven satire (Uncle Ramesh, Bro Winston, Yard Brief, Trini Brief, Bajan Brief, Rumour Mill, Patriots Portfolio, DJ Roadblock, Bam-Bam Sally, De Boys Seh, Back-a-Truck, Bounty Board, Guyanese Horizon, Progress Report)
- `satire` — pure satire pieces (non-column, fictional scenarios)
- `analysis` — optional (long-form, rare)

**Upgrade rule:** A post cannot be upgraded to `news` unless it passes the WHY IT MATTERS structure. No bulk upgrades. Selective upgrade of high-value posts only.

## Feedback loop (private, mandatory)

Internal tracking log:
- Headline
- WHY IT MATTERS call
- Date published
- Outcome check at 30 / 60 / 90 days
- Score: directionally right / partially right / wrong

Target: 70%+ directional accuracy. Below that = adjust approach.

## The moat

Not being right 100% of the time.

Being clearer, faster, and more consistently useful than anyone else.

Accuracy builds over time through feedback. Clarity must be present from day one.

## What we are NOT trying to be

- An economist
- A financial advisor
- A forecaster
- A newsroom
- A satire-only site
- A political debate platform

## What success looks like

The fastest, clearest explainer of cause → effect for diaspora readers.
