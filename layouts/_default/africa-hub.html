The Approach

What this coverage is for

Western outlets cover Africa in fragments — a crisis here, a World Cup there, an occasional music export. We cover four countries weekly, in depth, with voices that assume the context. Political analysis in Ghana and Kenya. Cultural mechanism reporting in Nigeria and South Africa. Not because these are the only African countries that matter, but because attention is finite and depth costs something. Four countries, done well, is our standard.
