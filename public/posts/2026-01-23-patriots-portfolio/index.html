The Guyana Stock Exchange had another week of trading, with consideration of $43.5M from 211,088 shares in 48 transactions. That’s up from last week! The market BELIEVES in Guyana!

1. RIVER CRUISE ADJACENT INVESTMENTS#

Minister Rodrigues just toured the “One Freedom” cruise ship. You know what this means? RIVER TOURISM IS COMING.

Patriot Play: Any business near the Demerara River that could benefit from tourist foot traffic. Think about it — 46 rooms of tourists need:

  • “Authentic Guyanese Experiences”

200 newly certified technicians entering the market. But you know what 200 technicians need?

  • Tools
  • Vehicles
  • Uniforms
  • Lunch

Patriot Play: The businesses that SERVICE the service providers. That’s where the smart money goes.

3. HINTERLAND AIRSTRIP ADJACENT#

Roraima Airways cutting fares 7% because of improved airstrips. More people flying = more economic activity in the hinterland.

Patriot Play: Lethem-adjacent anything. That border town about to BOOM.
