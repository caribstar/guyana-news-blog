🔥 HOT DEALS SPOTTED#

🥭 Mango Season Starting!#

Julie mangoes starting to show up! First batch going for $500-700 each, but give it two weeks and prices gon drop. De mango man by Bourda back gate say “Two more weeks and everybody eating mango!”

🧅 Onion Alert#

Onion prices DROP dis week! Was $600/lb last week, now seeing $450/lb in some spots. Stock up now — who knows what Budget gon bring!

🍚 Rice Deal#

5lb bag of rice going for $1,200 at de bulk shop on Regent Street. Dat’s $240/lb — good deal if you buying in quantity.

🛒 VENDOR SPOTLIGHT#

Miss Indra by Bourda Market Gate 3 selling de BEST ground provisions dis week. She tell me she getting fresh supply from Parika every morning. Eddoes, cassava, sweet potato — all looking good!

“Come early,” she say. “By 10 o’clock, de good stuff done sell out. People buying up everything before Budget.”

🚗 TRAFFIC ALERT FOR MARKET RUNS#

  • Stabroek area: BUSY! Park by de wharf and walk in
  • Bourda: Regent Street congested from 8am. Try coming from Camp Street side
  • Kitty Market: Less crowded option if you just need basics

💡 BACK-A-TRUCK TIPS#

  1. Bring yuh own bags — Vendors charging $50-100 for plastic bags now
  2. Small bills ready — Vendors don’t like breaking $5,000 notes for $300 purchase
  3. Go early — Best produce gone by 10am
  4. Bargain respectfully — Vendors remember good customers!
