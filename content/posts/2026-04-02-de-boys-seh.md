*De boys liming on the corner of Robb and Hincks. What deh say? Well...*

---

**On the tint crackdown:**

*"Bai, deh pull over every man except the one in de blacked-out government SUV. Dat one just drive straight through."*

*"De minister say 'don't call me.' She know exactly who does call."*

*"Listen, meh tint was 24% — one percent too dark. Deh fine meh thirty thousand dollars. Meanwhile de man who thief fourteen million in aircraft parts get bail in four hours."*

*"Thirty thousand for tint. Thirty thousand for illegal mining. This country have one price for everything, man."*

---

**On the Nicaraguan engineers and the missing $13 million:**

*"Dem resign Monday. Arrest Tuesday. Bail Tuesday night. Gone Wednesday. Dis is speedrun world record, bai."*

*"A prominent businessman visit the station for thirty minutes and suddenly dem get bail. Who dis businessman? Nobody know. Nobody asking. Interesting."*

*"Thirteen million in aircraft parts. Thirteen million! And de police say 'come back tomorrow at three.' Come back tomorrow? Bai, dem was packing before they reach home."*

*"De lawyer say in 23 years he never see dis. Twenty-three years. Dat is a man who practice under multiple governments and he NEVER see dis."*

---

**On Frederick Pollard coming home from Venezuela:**

*"Seven months in a Venezuelan jail. Seven months! And the GPF go quiet after one press release."*

*"Is de man own family find him. Not de police. Not de government. De family."*

*"His wife travel to Venezuela to bring him back. That woman strong, bai. Real strong."*

*"Welcome home, Frederick. Sorry it tek seven months and zero official help."*

---

**On the U.S. Ambassador defending the Exxon contract:**

*"She say renegotiating is a 'very bad idea.' Of course she say dat — she represent de country dat own Exxon."*

*"Fourteen-point-five percent. That is Guyana share of eleven-point-six billion barrels. Do de math."*

*"GHK Lall compare Exxon to Judas dis morning in de paper. Kaieteur News run it. Boldness."*

*"De Ambassador say 'just look around when I travel the country.' Bai, where exactly she travelling? Because Georgetown drain still flood."*

**On the Alberttown fire:**

*"Forty people on the street. Most of dem Cuban and Dominican. Nowhere to go."*

*"Fifty-seven rooms in dat building. Fifty-seven. That is a small community, not just a building."*

*"DHL staff pulling out documents during the blaze. Priorities, bai. Priorities."*

*"Investigation ongoing. Translation: nobody knows nothing and nobody going to find out nothing."*
