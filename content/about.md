## Who We Are (Or Pretend To Be)

Welcome to **The Daily Laugh** - Guyana's premier source of news commentary for people who've given up on being surprised by anything.

We're the blog that reads all four of Guyana's major newspapers every single day so you don't have to wonder which version of reality to believe. Spoiler alert: They're all right. And they're all wrong. Simultaneously. It's quantum journalism.

---

## What We Do

Every day, we:
1. Read **Stabroek News** (the intellectual one)
2. Read **Kaieteur News** (the one with ALL THE CAPS)
3. Read **Chronicle** (the government's BFF)
4. Read **Guyana Times** (the one that really wants you to know about beauty pageants)
5. Lose our minds slightly
6. Write jokes about it
7. Post it here
8. Wait for the angry comments

It's like being a news aggregator, except we add the one thing traditional journalism forgot: **humor**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are you real journalists?**  
A: No. We're satirists. There's a difference. Journalists have ethics committees. We have a delete button.

**Q: Do you hate Guyana?**  
A: We LOVE Guyana. That's why we care enough to mock its problems. If we didn't care, we'd just watch Netflix instead.

**Q: Why don't you write about [insert topic]?**  
A: Did it make the newspapers today? No? Then send us the story and we'll consider it for tomorrow's roundup.

**Q: Which newspaper do you work for?**  
A: None. We're independent, which means we're poor but honest.

**Q: Which political party do you support?**  
A: The Satire Party. Our platform is "everyone is ridiculous." We're undefeated in elections we haven't contested.

**Q: Can I share your posts?**  
A: Please do! Just give us credit. We need the validation.

**Q: Are you going to get sued?**  
A: Not if our lawyer has anything to say about it. And we have a really good [Legal Disclaimer](/legal/).

**Q: Do you accept submissions?**  
A: Not yet, but send us your funny takes on Guyana news anyway. We might steal them and give you credit. Maybe.

**Q: How do I contact you?**  
A: Scroll down. It's right there. We promise to read your emails, especially the angry ones. Those are the most entertaining.
