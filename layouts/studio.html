About

Hatchling Media is an independent IP studio developing original children’s and middle-grade story worlds for publishing, animation, and transmedia adaptation. We create creator-owned series built on strong educational, emotional, and franchise foundations.

Our model combines in-house IP creation with a rotating network of specialist collaborators across editorial, visual development, and story packaging.

Founder

Albert Massay

Creator & IP Lead

Albert Massay creates original children’s and middle-grade properties spanning animation, publishing, and educational storytelling. His work centers on culturally grounded settings, scalable story worlds, and series designed from the ground up for adaptation. Current and recent IP includes the Speedeet & Wilar Caribbean series and multiple properties in active development for publishing and screen.

Studio Model

Hatchling Media uses a studio-bench model: a flexible network of experienced creative specialists engaged per project. This allows each IP to be developed by the right experts while maintaining creator ownership and creative continuity across all properties.
