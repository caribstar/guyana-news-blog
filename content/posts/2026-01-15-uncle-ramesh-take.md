## Bai, Let Uncle Ramesh Explain De Math

So de census come out and Uncle Ramesh been doing some addition and subtraction—something dem government people apparently can't do. Or won't do. Uncle Ramesh suspect is de second one.

**Here's de problem:** Between 2012 and 2022:
- Population grow by 131,719 people
- Government build 92,233 new structures
- Each structure does hold about 3.23 people

Uncle Ramesh ain't no mathematician, but even he can calculate that 92,233 buildings times 3.23 people equal space for nearly 300,000 people. **Dat's MORE THAN TWICE de actual population increase.**

Uncle Ramesh been hearing about "housing crisis" since he born. But crisis is when you ain't got enough. What you call it when you build TWICE as much as you need? Uncle Ramesh call dat "spending spree with other people money."

---

## De Infrastructure Can't Keep Up

But here's de real joke, and Uncle Ramesh warning you—it ain't funny.

**All dem new houses?** Plenty ain't got:
- Reliable electricity (GPL struggling with 221 MW demand)
- Running water
- Proper road access

So government give people house lot, people celebrate, government take photo op, and den... nothing. De lot just sit there because you can't actually LIVE on it without power and water.

**De Wales Gas-to-Energy project** that supposed to fix de electricity problem? Delayed until late 2026. Uncle Ramesh will believe it when he see it. And he probably still won't see it.

## De Census Say What Now?

Uncle Ramesh favorite part of de census data:
- Building stock increased 42%
- Households increased 32.9%
- Population increased 17.6%

**Translation:** We building BUILDINGS faster than we making BABIES, and we creating HOUSEHOLDS faster than we getting PEOPLE.

Uncle Ramesh don't know what kind of economic model this is, but he pretty sure it ain't a good one. This is like buying three pairs of shoes for one foot. Technically you got shoes, but practically speaking, you just wasted money.

**And de Cybercrime Act?** Oh boy. Government using it to lock up people who complain about gun licenses, but de man on social media calling people all kind of nasty names getting free pass because he supporting de government.

Uncle Ramesh remember what dem smart people say: "The law, in its majestic equality, forbids the rich and poor alike to sleep under bridges, to beg in the streets, and to steal bread." Except in Guyana, de law also forbid you from criticizing de government, but it TOTALLY FINE to insult de opposition.

But Uncle Ramesh just a silly old man who think democracy involve "informing de public." What he know?
