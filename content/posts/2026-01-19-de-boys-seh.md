*Every Sunday, we check in with the boys at Rudy's Corner Shop to hear what regular Guyanese really thinking about the week's news.*

---

**Setting:** Rudy's Corner Shop, somewhere in Georgetown. Three plastic chairs, one wobbly table, and opinions stronger than the five-year-old rum Rudy selling as "premium."

**Present:** Big Man (56, taxi driver), Shorty (42, construction worker), Specs (38, government clerk who always have "inside information"), and Rambo (29, hustler who does "a little of everything")

---

## 🌞 DE BIG STORY: 37,000 Solar Panels

**Big Man:** Alright, who see de PM on dat podcast? Thirty-seven THOUSAND solar systems deliver to hinterland!

**Rambo:** Solar systems? Like... planets and ting?

**Specs:** *(sighs)* Solar PANELS, Rambo. For electricity. Not Saturn.

**Shorty:** Thirty-seven thousand! Dah's ah LOT of panels!

**Big Man:** Is ah lot of families getting light who never had light before. Imagine — you living in bush, no GPL line for miles, and now you could charge yuh phone and watch TV.

**Rambo:** Watch TV and see what? More politicians talking?

**Specs:** You does talk too much. De point is DEVELOPMENT reaching de interior. Before, dem villages was DARK after 6 PM. Now dem got power.

**Shorty:** Me cousin in Region Nine seh he finally got fan. FORTY YEARS he living there, and dis is de first time he sleeping cool.

**Big Man:** See? Dah's progress. Not everything need be controversy.

**Rambo:** But who PAYING for all dem panels?

**Specs:** Oil money! Dah's de whole point! We getting rich from de oil and spreading it around!

**Rambo:** Spreading it around to WHO though? I ain't see no panel on MY roof!

**Big Man:** You living in GEORGETOWN! You got GPL! De panel for people who CYAAN get GPL line!

**Rambo:** *(grumbling)* GPL don't mean you got GOOD electricity. Blackout every other day...

---

## 💰 BUDGET CONSULTATIONS: Who Get Invite?

**Shorty:** Speaking of money — government doing Budget consultations. PSC, Chamber of Commerce, everybody getting meeting.

**Specs:** Everybody except de opposition!

**Big Man:** And? What de opposition going contribute? "Boycott de budget"? "Investigate de budget"?

**Rambo:** To be fair, dem should at least get INVITE. Even if dem not going contribute nothing.

**Specs:** Why? So dem could leak everything to Kaieteur News and complain anyway? Dem get SAME budget document as everybody else when it present in Parliament.

**Shorty:** True. Private sector actually does BRING ideas. Tax proposals, investment plans. Opposition just bring complaints.

**Big Man:** Remember last year? PSC ask for construction equipment duty-free and dem get it. What de PNC ask for?

**Rambo:** *(laughing)* Recount!

**Specs:** See? Dah's why dem not in de room.

---

## 🏏 WOMEN CRICKET: DE REAL WINNERS

**Shorty:** But leh we talk bout something EVERYBODY could agree on — WOMEN'S CRICKET!

**Big Man:** SHEMAINE CAMPBELLE! Fifty not out! De woman is BOSS!

**Rambo:** Beat Jamaica comfortable! We undefeated in de T20 Blaze!

**Specs:** You know what sweet me? While everybody fighting bout politics and budget and solar panel, de women team just QUIETLY WINNING.

**Shorty:** No drama. No controversy. Just pure performance.

**Big Man:** Dah's how it suppose to be! You ever see Campbelle complaining on social media? Blaming umpire? Cussing fans?

**Rambo:** Never! She just go out there and BAT.

**Specs:** Meanwhile, some men's team players does spend more time on Instagram than in de nets.

**Big Man:** *(stands up)* A TOAST! To de Guyana women's team — de REAL champions!

**All:** 🍻 TO DE WOMEN!

---

## 🏗️ AIRSTRIP NEWS: $993 MILLION IN KARASABAI

**Shorty:** Dem starting dat airstrip in Karasabai tomorrow! $993 million project!

**Rambo:** How much?!

**Specs:** You hear right. Close to ah BILLION dollars for one airstrip.

**Big Man:** But is not JUST airstrip. Is fifty jobs for local people. Is connection to de rest of de country. Is medical evacuation when emergency happen.

**Rambo:** Still though... billion dollars could build ah LOT of things in Georgetown.

**Specs:** Georgetown GETTING things. De bridge, de highway, de new hospital. Dis is for people who never had NOTHING.

**Shorty:** Me father-in-law from Region Eight. He seh before, if you get sick bad-bad, you might DEAD before you reach hospital. Now with airstrip, you could get airlift out.

**Big Man:** Dah's de difference between life and death. Cyaan put price on dat.

**Rambo:** *(quietly)* Alright, alright. When you put it dat way...

---

## 🤔 DE FINAL WORD

**Big Man:** So what we learn dis week?

**Shorty:** Solar panel bright, opposition not so bright.

**Specs:** Women cricketers winning, men politicians still fighting.

**Rambo:** And apparently Georgetown people like me not getting free panel because we "already got GPL."

**Big Man:** You want free panel? MOVE to Lethem!

**Rambo:** Me? In de SUN? Bai, I does melt if de temperature go past 30.

**Shorty:** *(laughing)* Dah's why you not getting panel! You would short-circuit it wid yuh sweat!

**Big Man:** Alright, alright. Same time next week?

**All:** Same time, same place. 

**Rambo:** And Rudy — next time bring COLD drinks nah? Dis lukewarm ting killing me!

**Rudy:** *(from behind counter)* Fridge running on GPL! Blame DEM!

*(Everyone laughs as de Sunday sun sets on another week in Guyana)*
