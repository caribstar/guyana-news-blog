DJ Roadblock: Weekend Traffic Report & Road Vibes

January 23, 2026
From The Tradewinds Brief — daily diaspora reporting. Subscribe Free →

MASSIVE FRIDAYS with DJ Roadblock! Your traffic and vibes connection! 🎧🚗

🚨 FRIDAY EVENING TRAFFIC UPDATE#

Wha gwan massive! DJ Roadblock in de building, bringing you de Friday rush hour situation!

GEORGETOWN:

  • Sheriff Street: HEAVY as always on a Friday evening. Patience is a virtue, people!
  • Vlissengen Road: Moving but slow near the Seawall
  • East Bank Highway: Expect delays heading out of town — everybody trying to reach home!
  • Mandela Avenue: Moderate traffic, watch out for the usual Evening News crowd

EAST COAST:

  • Traffic building up from Georgetown to Buxton
  • The road good though — just plenty vehicles

WEST COAST/WEST BANK:

  • Demerara Harbour Bridge: Normal wait times
  • Traffic flowing both ways

🚧 ROAD WORKS TO KNOW ABOUT#

Stanleytown Four-Lane Bridge: The opposition raising concerns about this delayed project causing traffic, dust, and affecting businesses. If you passing that way, expect some disruption.

Black Bush Polder Access Road: Rehabilitation works ongoing. Drive careful if you heading into the Polder area.

Aubrey Barker Road Connection: Works continuing to connect to South Ruimveldt. Some delays in that area.

🎉 WEEKEND ROAD VIBES#

Saturday:

  • Market traffic early morning — Stabroek, Bourda, Mon Repos
  • Expect beach traffic if weather nice — Seawall going be packed!

Sunday:

  • Church morning traffic
  • Afternoon family time traffic
  • Evening lime traffic picking up

🔊 DJ ROADBLOCK TIP OF THE WEEK#

“If you drinking this weekend, DON’T DRIVE. Call a friend, call a taxi, call your aunty who don’t drink. Just don’t be the headline Monday morning.”

📻 REQUEST LINE OPEN!#

Stay safe out there, massive! DJ Roadblock signing off until next Friday!
