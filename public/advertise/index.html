Who Should Advertise?#

We welcome advertisers from:

  • Financial services — Banks, credit unions, remittance services, insurance
  • Real estate — Guyana and diaspora property markets
  • Travel and tourism — Airlines, hotels, tour operators
  • Legal services — Immigration, business law, family law
  • Retail and e-commerce — Products serving the Guyanese market
  • Food and beverage — Restaurants, Caribbean food brands
  • Technology — Apps and services for the Caribbean market
  • Events — Concerts, cultural events, community gatherings
  • Professional services — Accounting, consulting, healthcare

Advertising Guidelines#

To maintain our readers’ trust, we have a few guidelines:

  1. Clearly labeled: All sponsored content is marked as advertising
  2. Relevant: Ads should be relevant to our Guyanese and Caribbean audience
  3. Appropriate: We reserve the right to decline ads that conflict with our values
  4. No political ads: We do not accept advertising from political parties or candidates
