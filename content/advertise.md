## Advertising Guidelines

To maintain our readers' trust, we have a few guidelines:

1. **Clearly labeled:** All sponsored content is marked as advertising
2. **Relevant:** Ads should be relevant to our Guyanese and Caribbean audience
3. **Appropriate:** We reserve the right to decline ads that conflict with our values
4. **No political ads:** We do not accept advertising from political parties or candidates
