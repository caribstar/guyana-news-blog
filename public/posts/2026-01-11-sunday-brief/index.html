📊 SUNDAY QUICK STATS#

🎭 THE MOHAMEDS PRESENT: “THEY’RE NOT GOING ANYWHERE” (THE MUSICAL)#

This week, the Mohamed legal saga reached peak absurdity. Let’s recap the highlights:

This Week’s Timeline:

  • Monday: Acting Chief Justice says “No stay”
  • Monday (5 minutes later): Defense appeals to Full Court
  • Tuesday: Courtroom drama with first witness
  • Thursday: More drama with “late evidence”
  • Friday: Nazar’s op-ed declares “government declared war on his family”

Defense Attorney’s Battle Cry: “The Mohameds are NOT going anywhere.”

Translation: “We will appeal until the heat death of the universe.”

The Grievances (According to Nazar’s Op-Ed):

  1. Insurance won’t cover his bulletproof vehicles (The AUDACITY!)
  2. GRA employees who helped him are being arrested (for helping commit fraud, but details!)
  3. His lands are being “illegally occupied” by squatters (more on this later)
  4. Government using “political influence” (says the guy facing fraud charges)

Kaieteur News Coverage: Has written more words about the Mohameds than Shakespeare wrote plays. They could publish a 47-volume encyclopedia at this point.

The Pattern: Court says no → Lawyers appeal → Court says no again → Lawyers appeal the appeal → Repeat until Florida gives up

Florida’s Patience Level: Approaching absolute zero

🏗️ HEROES HIGHWAY: THE SEQUEL NOBODY ASKED FOR#

Remember Heroes Highway? The concrete road commissioned December 2023? It needs repairs already!

Government: “We always planned to put asphalt over the concrete!”

Everyone: “Then why use concrete at all?”

Government: crickets

The Timeline:

  • December 2023: Highway commissioned
  • March 2025: US Secretary Rubio says “we almost had concussions”
  • April 2025: Ali announces overlay was “always the plan”
  • January 2026: Bids invited for asphalt overlay

Original Cost: $13.9 billion (12 contractors)

Additional Cost: Millions more for asphalt

What We Could’ve Done: Built proper asphalt road from start

What We Did: Speed-run concrete, then fix with asphalt

📈 CRIME STATS: THE NUMBERS GAME#

The Police released 2025 stats. Depending on which metric you pick, Guyana is either safer or deadlier.

Good News: Serious crimes down 25.5% (from 1,235 to 920 cases)

Bad News: 130 murders + 137 road deaths = 267 people died violently

Government’s Press Release: “Significant crime reduction!”

Reality: More people were murdered and killed on roads than 2024.

The Spin vs The Truth: Focus on percentages, ignore the body count.

🤷 THE OPPOSITION: STILL MISSING#

Parliament convened in November. Still no Opposition Leader. Why? Azruddin Mohamed (WIN’s leader with 16 seats) is fighting extradition.

WIN’s Warning: “No Opposition Leader, No Oversight”

The Irony: The presumptive Opposition Leader is too busy fighting fraud charges to lead.

Kaieteur’s Savage Quote: “It’s almost as if the opposition is in [the PPP’s] fold, under its control”

Expected Resolution: February 5… or whenever appeals finish… so maybe 2030?

🏡 SQUATTER GATE: MOHAMED’S LAND DRAMA#

Nazar Mohamed claims squatters invaded his East Bank Demerara land near Heroes Highway. He alleges government officials encouraged them.

His Response: “I will demolish any structures they build.”

The Squatters: “Someone said Mohamed gave out the land.”

The Twist: Police who responded were allegedly transferred immediately.

The Conspiracy Theory: Political vindictiveness over extradition

Reality: Nobody knows, but it’s great drama

⚡ GPL: CONTRACTORS STILL CAN’T SEE POWER LINES#
