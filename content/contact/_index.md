We read everything that comes in. Here's where to send it.

## Editorial

Tips, story leads, corrections, letters to the editor, or general editorial correspondence:

**[editorial@tradewindsbrief.com](mailto:editorial@tradewindsbrief.com)**

If you are flagging a factual error in something we published, please include the post URL and a description of the issue. We respond to legitimate correction requests within two business days.

## Business

Reprint and syndication permissions, partnership inquiries, advertising, or anything commercial:

**[business@tradewindsbrief.com](mailto:business@tradewindsbrief.com)**

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within two business days. Editorial corrections are prioritized.

## What We Do Not Do

We do not accept paid placement of editorial content. We do not write sponsored posts disguised as commentary. We do not coordinate coverage with political parties, campaigns, or government offices. If you are reaching out with one of those requests, we will decline politely.
