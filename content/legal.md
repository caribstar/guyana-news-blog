### How We Avoid Defamation

Under Guyana's defamation law, we protect ourselves by:

**1. Truth Defense**
- Our satirical exaggerations are clearly not meant as fact
- Reasonable readers understand satire is not literal truth
- We base commentary on actual published news stories

**2. Fair Comment Defense**
- We comment on matters of genuine public interest
- Our opinions are honestly held
- We base commentary on true facts from published sources
- Public officials and figures are subject to greater scrutiny

**3. Reasonable Publication**
- Our satire targets public conduct, not private lives
- We focus on matters of legitimate public concern
- We don't make false factual allegations
- Context makes clear our content is humor/opinion
